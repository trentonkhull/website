Trenton K. Hull

Veteran crooner Trenton Hull has built his music career in Dallas/Ft. Worth
as a man with more than a couple hats, performing with pop and party bands,
playing small jazz venues, playing synthesizers in electronic bands,
and he is a regular Dallas-area studio singer. He’s sung background vocals for Ben Folds,
and Sarah Brightman. He still draws on memories from his time at the University of North Texas,
where he studied Vocal Jazz, and was named Downbeat Magazine’s Undergrad Vocalist of the Year in 2012.
For Trenton, nothing beats the thrill of making music with any of the amazingly talented Dallas musicians
he’s come to know as friends.
